Founding product designer at a web3 events and content platform — Luma-style events meets Substack-style writing, focused on crypto, web3, and AI. Small product team operating in fast iteration mode while hunting for PMF / MRR.

Goals: Establish a brand identity and creative direction from zero. Build a flexible, scalable design system that could keep pace with shifting product strategy. Drive consistency and velocity across a small team without dedicated design ops.

Outcomes: Brand identity system built end-to-end — guidelines, visual language, social/asset direction. Design system shipped in Figma and translated to code via Claude Code + Storybook. System adopted in-product, raising consistency and shipping speed.

Failures: PMF still unresolved — product direction shifted multiple times, which forced rework on brand applications and component coverage. Monetization and recurring revenue remain open problems.
