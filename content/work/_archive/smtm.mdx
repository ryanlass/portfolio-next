## Role / Scope

Mobile app design for a content creator and influencer platform, delivered through Decimal Studios as an L&F contract engagement. Product goal: give creators direct ownership and control over their content — easy creation, updates, and management without platform friction.

## Goals

- Design a mobile-first product that puts content control back in creators' hands.
- Prioritize simplicity in core flows (create, update, manage) for a non-technical user base.
- Deliver under an agency-of-record model with Decimal driving client relationship.
