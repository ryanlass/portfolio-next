Design system lead and visual/UI design lead at an enterprise no-code builder — Alphabet-backed unicorn, pre-AI era (drag-and-drop, no Claude/Codex). Team leaned heavily UX/research; brought in to elevate craft and own the system.

Goals: Build, refine, and lead a multi-pillar design system from scratch alongside a dedicated dev team. Cover the breadth of a complex enterprise platform with enough flexibility to support distinct product areas. Raise the visual and UI bar on a research-heavy team.

Outcomes: Multi-pillar design system shipped and integrated into product. Clear lift in craft, consistency, and product experience across the platform. Established a working model between design and engineering for ongoing system contribution.
