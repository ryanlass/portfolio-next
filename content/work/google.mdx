Brand and content system design for Google's internal UX content hub, delivered via Decimal Studios. GoUX served Googlers as a central destination for articles, videos, podcasts, and tutorials — inspiration, knowledge, and craft support for the broader UX organization.

Goals: Establish a brand identity for the hub that read as distinctly Google but distinct from product UI. Design a flexible content system to handle multiple media types and a steady editorial cadence. Make discovery and consumption feel intentional, not like an internal wiki.

Outcomes: Brand identity and content system shipped. Hub launched with structured support for articles, video, audio, and tutorials. Internal-facing destination established for the UX org.
